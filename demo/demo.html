MultipleDatePicker demo page

We use here some functions and variables from module scope to provide exemples always available for the current month, please check app.js to know what is inside.

Don't forget to include the dependency in your app

var app = angular.module('myApp', ['multipleDatePicker']);

No options, no callback, 50% screen width

<div style="width: 50%">
      <multiple-date-picker/>
</div>

Callback open alert with infos

<div style="width: 25%">
      <multiple-date-picker callback="logInfos(timestamp, selected);"/>
</div>

Don't want to work Sundays and Wednesdays ?

<div style="width: 25%">
      <multiple-date-picker week-days-off="[0, 3]" callback="logInfos(timestamp, selected);"/>
</div>

14th is bank holiday, can't use that day

<div style="width: 25%">
      <multiple-date-picker days-off="oneDayOff" callback="logInfos(timestamp, selected);"/>
</div>

4th, 5th and 8th already selected

<div style="width: 25%">
      <multiple-date-picker days-selected="selectedDays"/>
</div>

Combine everything \o/

<div style="width: 25%">
      <multiple-date-picker days-off="oneDayOff" days-selected="selectedDays" week-days-off="[0]" callback="logInfos(timestamp, selected);"/>
</div>
